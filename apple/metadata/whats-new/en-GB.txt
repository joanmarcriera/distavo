• The menu-bar icon now shows what Distavo is doing at a glance: recording, loading, transcribing, or a new note ready to read.
• Transcription language is now a dropdown — pick from Auto-detect and 100 languages instead of typing a code, so a typo can't quietly break transcription.
• More reliable connections to your own transcription and summary servers: a new "Check permissions…" helper spots and fixes a macOS Local Network permission that could quietly go stale after an update.
• Clearer first-run experience: if Ollama isn't running yet, Test Connections now explains what still works and how to finish setup instead of showing unexplained red dots.
• Local Network permission made simple: the permissions helper tells you when the permission actually applies, and a new "Request Access Now" button makes macOS ask immediately.
