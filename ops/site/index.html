Meeting notes without handing meetings to someone else's cloud.

Distavo is the thin native Mac layer around your own transcription and summarisation setup. It watches, converts, calls your configured endpoints, validates, and writes notes locally.

Watch

Use the folder you already sync.

Point Distavo at a local, iCloud Drive, or Google Drive folder. When a recording finishes syncing, Distavo processes it once.

Transcribe

Send audio only where you decide.

AVFoundation converts media locally, then Distavo uploads the WAV to the WhisperX endpoint you configured.

Summarise

Write Markdown notes with your model.

The cleaned transcript goes to your Ollama server, then Distavo writes a structured note to your notes folder.

Native settings. No local web panel.

The App Store build is sandboxed and uses security-scoped bookmarks for folders. Direct and Setapp builds keep the same core pipeline with edition-specific distribution behavior.

Configure server URLs, models, speaker options, folders, and local fallback from the Mac settings window. The app has no telemetry and no phone-home.
